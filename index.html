Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the moon sometimes out during the day?

The moon is sometimes out during the day because it orbits the Earth and can be visible in the sky at different times.

Why is the sky blue?

The sky is blue because molecules in the air scatter blue light from the sun more than they scatter red light.

Will we ever discover aliens?

The possibility of discovering aliens depends on many factors including advancements in technology and space exploration.

How much does the Earth weigh?

The Earth weighs approximately 5.97 x 10^24 kilograms.

How do airplanes stay up?

Airplanes stay up by generating lift through their wings and propulsion from their engines.
